# Optimize

Purpose: Optimize contains optimisation routines for OPF, unit commitment, and market-clearing algorithms.

Responsibilities:

- DC/AC OPF formulations and solvers
- Unit commitment (MILP) helpers and heuristics
- SCOPF and multi-period optimisation
- Integration with LP/MILP solvers (OxiZ)

Used by microgrid EMS and market simulation examples.
